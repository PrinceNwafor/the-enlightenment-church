Statement of Faith

What We Believe

Our beliefs are rooted in the eternal, unchanging Word of God. These truths are not opinions — they are the bedrock on which every life at TEC Global is built.

01
The Holy Scriptures
We believe the Bible is the inspired, infallible, and authoritative Word of God. Every word of Scripture is God-breathed and profitable for doctrine, correction, and instruction in righteousness. It is the final authority in all matters of faith and practice. (2 Timothy 3:16–17)
02
The Holy Trinity
We believe in one God, eternally existing in three persons: the Father, the Son (Jesus Christ), and the Holy Spirit — co-equal, co-eternal, and co-existent. Each person is fully God, and there is only one God. (Matthew 28:19; 2 Corinthians 13:14)
03
The Lord Jesus Christ
We believe in the deity of our Lord Jesus Christ — that He is God manifest in the flesh, born of a virgin, lived a sinless life, died as an atoning sacrifice for the sins of all mankind, rose bodily from the dead, ascended to the right hand of the Father, and will return in power and glory. (John 1:1–14; Philippians 2:5–11)
04
Salvation by Faith in Christ Alone
We believe salvation is a gift of God's grace received through faith in Jesus Christ alone — not by works, merit, or religious performance. Everyone who believes in Jesus is immediately made righteous, forgiven, and granted eternal life. (Ephesians 2:8–9; Romans 10:9–10)
05
The New Creation
We believe that every born-again believer is a new creation in Christ Jesus — the old has passed away and all things have become new. The New Creation is not a product of natural birth, but of the Spirit of God. Understanding this truth is foundational to living as God intended. (2 Corinthians 5:17)
06
In Christ Realities
We believe that the believer's position "in Christ" is one of unimaginable privilege, authority, and blessing. In Christ, we are righteous, redeemed, healed, loved, and complete. These are not future realities — they are present facts for every child of God. (Ephesians 1:3–14; Colossians 2:10)
07
The Holy Spirit
We believe in the present-day ministry of the Holy Spirit, who indwells every believer, gives spiritual gifts, empowers for service, and guides into all truth. We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit with evidence and the ongoing gifts of the Spirit for the Church today. (Acts 1:8; 1 Corinthians 12)
08
Divine Healing
We believe that healing — physical, emotional, and mental — was provided in the atonement of Jesus Christ. By His stripes we were healed. God desires wholeness for every person, and healing is part of our inheritance in Christ. (Isaiah 53:5; 1 Peter 2:24; James 5:14–15)
09
The Resurrection and Return of Christ
We believe in the bodily resurrection of Jesus Christ from the dead and in the promised, literal, personal return of Jesus Christ to this earth. We live in the light of His coming and build the Kingdom until He returns. (1 Thessalonians 4:16–17; Revelation 19:11–16)
10
The Church as the Body of Christ
We believe the Church is the Body of Christ on earth — called to represent Jesus, make disciples, demonstrate the Kingdom, and be the light of the world. TEC Global exists to be that church: enlightened, equipped, and engaged for God's purposes. (Matthew 16:18; Ephesians 4:11–16)

"That the God of our Lord Jesus Christ, the Father of glory, may give to you the spirit of wisdom and revelation in the knowledge of Him."

Ephesians 1:17 · #InChristRealities
